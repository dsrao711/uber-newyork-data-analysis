### Conclusion:
From the analysis , we can conclude that , 
- Maximum customers are during the evening time because of peak working hours
- Rush increases from 7pm to 9pm
- Maximum customers are noticed on month ends
- Maximum customers are notices on Sundays
- Maximum rise in the number of customers has been noticed from July to September
